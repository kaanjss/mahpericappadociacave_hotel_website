Sureyya Hammam Cave Room

Sureyya, takes its name from the children of a close engineer friend who supported us during the design stage—an elegant reminder of the friendships and effort behind this hotel. Set within authentic cave architecture, natural rock textures and soft lighting bring Cappadocia’s timeless atmosphere indoors. The private hammam feature turns your stay into a soothing ritual of relaxation, while the room’s signature detail—a round bed—adds a truly distinctive, romantic touch, making the experience feel almost storybook. A perfect choice for guests seeking calm, comfort, and a “one-of-a-kind” Cappadocia memory.
